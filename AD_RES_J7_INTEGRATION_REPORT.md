**Description:** Directors protected for decisions made in good faith, with reasonable information, without conflict

**Application:** Jax/Dan IT expense decisions protected by s76(4) Companies Act

**Consequence:** Peter must rebut presumption of good faith by proving fraud/gross negligence

**Supporting Lex:** business-judgment-rule, rational-basis-test, good-faith-presumption

### 4. Manufactured Crisis Framework (Company Law)

**Description:** When party creates problem they complain about, indicates bad faith and ulterior motive

**Application:** Peter cancelled cards making documentation inaccessible, then complained about missing documentation

**Consequence:** Rebuts good faith presumption, establishes bad faith, improper purpose, abuse of process

**Indicators:**
- Actor created problem complained about
- Timing suspicious (day after Dan provided reports)
- Disproportionate response (ex parte interdict)
- No internal resolution attempt
- Problem serves actor's interests
- Coordination with settlement negotiation

**Supporting Lex:** manufactured-crisis-indicators, timing-analysis-bad-faith, obstruction-of-documentation-indicators

**Where to Add:** PARA 7.2-7.5, PARA 7.14-7.15, Bad faith analysis

### 8. Unjust Enrichment (Four-Element Test) (Civil Law)

**Description:** RWD unjustly enriched at expense of RegimA Zone Ltd through unauthorized platform usage

**Consequence:** All four elements satisfied, unjust enrichment established, restitution due

**Four-Element Test:**
- **Enrichment:** RWD saved R140K-R280K, received services valued R2.94M-R6.88M
- **At Expense Of:** RegimA Zone Ltd paid R140K-R280K, patrimony decreased
- **No Legal Ground:** No contract, no gift intention, no statutory entitlement, no court order
- **No Defense:** Cannot claim change of position, estoppel, or bona fide purchase

**Restitution:**
- **Minimum:** R140K-R280K (actual cost)
- **Market Value:** R2.94M-R6.88M (market rate)
- **Quantum Meruit:** Reasonable remuneration for services

**Supporting Lex:** unjust-enrichment-four-element-test, expense-saved-enrichment, quantum-meruit

**Where to Add:** Comprehensive unjust enrichment claim, Counter-application, RWD analysis
