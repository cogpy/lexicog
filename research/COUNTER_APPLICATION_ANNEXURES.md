## 3. Detailed Description of Each Annexure

*   **Significance:** This is the "smoking gun" that proves the act of sabotage. It directly links Peter to the crippling of the company's financial operations and establishes the retaliatory motive.

### CA-2: Timeline of Events (June 6-7, 2025)
*   **Content:** A detailed, minute-by-minute timeline of events on June 6 and 7, 2025, cross-referenced with evidence.
*   **Significance:** This timeline will be a powerful narrative tool to demonstrate the cause-and-effect relationship between Dan's whistleblowing and Peter's retaliation.

### CA-3: Peter's Founding Affidavit
*   **Content:** The full text of Peter's founding affidavit, with annotations highlighting the material non-disclosures.
*   **Significance:** This document is a direct admission of the misrepresentations made to the court.

### CA-4: Financial Records (SLG & Unallocated Expenses)
*   **Content:** Audited financial statements, internal management accounts, and journal entries related to the R5.4 million stock adjustment and the unallocated expenses.
*   **Significance:** This evidence will demonstrate the scale of the financial mismanagement and the resulting harm to the company.

### CA-5: Faucitt Family Trust Deed
*   **Content:** The complete and signed Faucitt Family Trust deed.
*   **Significance:** This will establish Peter's fiduciary duties as a Trustee and show how he breached those duties by acting against the interests of a beneficiary.

*   **Content:** Loan agreements, trust documents, and correspondence proving Bantjies's multiple conflicting roles.
*   **Significance:** This evidence will destroy Bantjies's credibility as an independent witness and expose the collusion between him and Peter.

### CA-7: Revenue Stream Hijacking Documentation

### CA-8: Court Order for Kayla's Email Account
*   **Content:** The court order and any related documents.
*   **Significance:** This will show a pattern of using the legal system for improper purposes and interfering with other legal processes.

### CA-9: Dan's Comprehensive Reports to Bantjies
*   **Content:** The full set of reports and supporting documents submitted by Dan to Bantjies on June 6, 2025.
*   **Significance:** This proves that Peter and his associates were fully aware of the allegations of misconduct before they took retaliatory action.

### CA-10: Communications with SARS

*   **Significance:** This will demonstrate the real-world consequences of Peter's gross negligence, including potential penalties and reputational damage.
