## Evidence Organization Guidelines

### 1. Document Formatting Standards

**For each annexure:**
- Create a cover page with annexure number (CA-1, CA-2, etc.)
- Include a table of contents if the annexure contains multiple documents
- Number all pages consecutively
- Use clear headers and footers with case number and annexure reference

### 2. Image and OCR Standards

**For images and scanned documents:**
- Attach images to their OCR text extractions
- Format as a single document with the annexure name
- Ensure OCR text is searchable and accurate
- Include both the original image and the extracted text

### 3. Email Evidence Standards

**For email correspondence:**
- Include full email headers (From, To, Date, Subject)
- Preserve email chains to show context
- Highlight key passages relevant to the allegations
- Verify timestamps are clearly visible

### 4. Financial Document Standards

**For financial statements and records:**
- Include complete financial statements (not excerpts)
- Provide comparative periods where relevant
- Include any auditor's notes or qualifications
- Attach supporting schedules and journal entries

### 5. Legal Document Standards

**For court orders and legal documents:**
- Include complete documents (not summaries)
- Ensure all signatures and stamps are clearly visible
- Include any related correspondence or applications
- Verify all dates and case numbers are accurate
